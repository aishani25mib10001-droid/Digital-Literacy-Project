## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

### ✅ Do’s
- Use strong and secure passwords  
- Think before posting anything online  
- Respect others and communicate politely  
- Keep your privacy settings updated  
- Verify information before sharing  

### ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information  
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation  
- Avoid using offensive or inappropriate language  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Do not overshare your personal life online
