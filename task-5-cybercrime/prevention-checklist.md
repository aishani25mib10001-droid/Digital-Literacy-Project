## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

### ✅ General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.  
- Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords online.  
- Think before clicking on links or downloading files from unknown sources.  
- Avoid accepting friend requests or messages from unknown people.  
- Keep your social media privacy settings updated.  
- Be careful about what you post online, as it can affect your reputation.  
- Always log out from public or shared devices.

### 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept payment requests from strangers.  

### 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
If you face any cybercrime, report it immediately:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
